Our Mission

TravlerPack aims to keep refugees and displaced persons warm during the winter. We designed our sleeping bag to serve as a long-term, cost-effective alternative to traditional methods of staying warm such as burning wood and fuel.

 

Our Impact

In 2018 and 2019, we distributed 1,000 TravlerPacks to Syrian refugees in Idlib, Syria. In 2020, we distributed another 140 TravlerPacks to Syrian refugees in Beqaa Valley. In 2021, we distributed another 200 TravlerPacks to Syrian refugees in Idlib, Syria. In 2023, we distributed 2,135 TravlerPacks in Idlib, Syria. These distributions have been facilitated by two nonprofit partners: NuDay Syria and The Kayany Foundation.

In October 2023, we plan to distribute another 2,000 TravlerPacks in the Beqaa Valley, Lebanon and Gaza Camp, Jordan with two additional nonprofit partners.
